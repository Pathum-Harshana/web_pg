NFTH Flood Reliefs at Kaduwela Walivita

Dr Neville Fernando Teaching Hospital (NFTH) is the largest private hospital and also the first & only private teaching hospital in the country. Our CSR arm is called “NFTH CSR” and into many CSR activities such as recently introduced “NFTH CSR Non Paying patients”. “NFTH Flood Reliefs” is another initiative of “NFTH CSR” to look after the flood victims of Kaduwela Walivita area.
